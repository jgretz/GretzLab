Corn Casserole

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • .5 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 (8.5 ounce) pkg dry corn bread mix
  • 1 (15 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained
  • 1 (14.75 ounce) can creamed corn
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350
  2. Melt butter
  3. Beat eggs
  4. Lightly grease 9x9 pan
  5. Combine all ingredients
  6. Bake for 45 minutes or until the top is golden brown.
