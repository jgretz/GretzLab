Biscuits and Gravy

Yields 8-10 Servings

Biscuits

Ingredients

  • 2.5 cup self rising flour
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • .5 tsp kosher salt
  • 4 tbsp vegetable shortening
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup buttermilk

Instructions

  1. reheat oven to 450
  2. Whish flour, sugar, and salt
  3. Fold in shortening and butter (less handling the better = fluffy biscuits)
  4. Pour in butter milk and mix (don’t over mix)
  5. Flour surface
  6. Fold over itself 2 or 3 times, make into a mound, and roll out
  7. Cut biscuits (don’t twist), flour biscuit cutter in between if needed
  8. Place on cookie sheet
  9. Bake 15-18 minutes

Sausage for Gravy

Ingredients

  • 2 lb pork
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1.5 tsp pepper
  • 2 tsp sage
  • 2 tsp thyme
  • .5 tsp rosemary
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • .5 tsp cayenne
  • .25 tsp red pepper

Instructions

  1. Grind Meat
  2. Mix Spices
  3. Lightly Brown

Gravy

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Sausage
  • .25 cup Diced Onion
  • 6 tbsp Flour
  • 4 cups Whole Milk
  • .5 tsp Poultry Seasoning
  • .5 tsp Nutmeg
  • .25 tsp Salt
  • 1 dash Worcestershire
  • 1 dash Tabasco
  • 1 tbsp Butter

Instructions

  1. Add onion to a little of the sausage grease (or a little butter), stir until transparent
  2. Add sausage
  3. Add flour and butter, stir 6-8 minutes, until you have a "sausage roux"
  4. Add seasoning and stir until mixed
  5. Add milk
  6. Stir until thickened
