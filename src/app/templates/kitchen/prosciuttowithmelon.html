Prosciutto with Melon

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 Honeydew Melon
  • 1 package Prosciutto di Parma
  • .5 tsp lime juice
  • .5 tsp crushed red flakes
  • .25 tsp Kosher salt
  • 2 mint leaves

Instructions

  1. Ball Melon
  2. Mix lime juice, red pepper flakes, salt, mint (reserve 1/2 leaf of mint for step 5)
  3. Lightly toss in mixture
  4. Wrap in prosciutto
  5. Sprinkle remaining mint
  6. Drizzle olive oil
