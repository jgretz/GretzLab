Collard Greens

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 bunch collard greens
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp tabasco
  • .5 lb ham steak with bone

Instructions

  1. Wash and cut greens into strips
  2. Bring water to boil
  3. Add meat and spices, reduce heat to medium (just below a boil), let go for an hour
  4. Add greens and butter
  5. Cook until desired tender but firm, stirring occasionally
