Bread Pudding

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1.5 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar
  • .5 tsp nutmeg
  • .25 cup butter
  • 2.75 cup whipping cream
  • 4 cup cubed bread
  • .75 cup raisin

Instructions

  1. Cube bread, toast in oven
  2. Grease pan
  3. Put in bread
  4. Mix in raisins
  5. Mix the rest together, then pour over bread, distributing evenly
  6. Bake @ 375 for 30 â€“ 45 minutes, until brown

Vanilla Sauce

Ingredients

  • .5 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp all purpose flour
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp nutmeg
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1.25 cup whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp vanilla

Instructions

  1. Mix everything but the vanilla
  2. Cook over medium, whisking constantly
  3. Remove when thick (10-12 minutes)
  4. Stir in vanilla
