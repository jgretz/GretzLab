Prosciutto Wrapped Greens

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 package Prosciutto di Parma
  • .25 lb Arugala
  • 2 tbs parmigiano reggiano
  • 3 tsp olive oil
  • 2 tsp red wine vinegar
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cracked black pepper
  • .5 tsp Dijon Mustard

Instructions

  1. Mix olive oil, vinegar, lemon juice, dijon mustard
  2. Wash arugala
  3. Toss arugala with parmigiano reggiano, salt, pepper
  4. Toss with mixture from step 1
  5. Roll greens, cut, wrap in prosciutto
