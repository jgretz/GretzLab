Buttermilk Fried Chicken

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 lbs Chicken Breast Strips

Milk Bath

  • 2 cups Buttermilk
  • 6 cloves Garlic (crushed)
  • 1 Onion (diced)
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • .5 tsp Paprika

Breading

  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tbsp Seasoning Salt
  • .5 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • .5 tsp Paprika
  • .25 tsp Onion Powder
  • .25 tsp Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Put in milk bath overnight (at least 8 hours)
  2. Remove chicken, remove excess milk
  3. Bread chicken
  4. Let sit (refrigerated) for 30-60 minutes
  5. Fry in oil @ 350
