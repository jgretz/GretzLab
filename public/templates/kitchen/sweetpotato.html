Sweet Potato Casserole

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 3 cup sweet potato (about 5 lb of sweet potato)
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • .5 cup milk
  • .5 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400
  2. Place sweet potatoes directly on rack, roast for about 1 hour, until inside is firm, but malleable (place tray or foil underneath rack to protect oven)
  3. Remove potatoes, let rest for 15 minutes
  4. Peel potatoes
  5. Mash potatoes
  6. Melt Butter
  7. Mix all
  8. Sprinkle brown sugar lightly on top
