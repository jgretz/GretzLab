Country Fried Steak

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 lb Steak (lesser cut ok)
  • 2 cup Flour
  • 1 tbsp Baking Powder
  • 1 tbsp Seasoning Salt
  • .5 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • .5 tsp Paprika
  • .25 tsp Onion Powder
  • .25 tsp Garlic Powder

Instructions

  1. Tenderize steak with meat mallet
  2. Bread steak
  3. Let sit for 30 minutes
  4. Fry in oil @ 350
