Strawberry Pretzel Salad

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cup crushed pretzels
  • .75 cup butter
  • 3 tbsp white sugar
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 8 oz whipped topping
  • 2 cup boiling water
  • 6 oz strawberry jello mix
  • 10 oz frozen strawberries

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Stir together crushed pretzels, melted butter and 3 tablespoons sugar
  3. Grease pan with spray, pack pretzel mixture in
  4. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, until set. Set aside to cool.
  5. Mix cream cheese and 1 cup sugar. Fold in whipped topping. Spread mixture onto cooled crust.
  6. Dissolve gelatin in boiling water. Stir in still frozen strawberries and allow to set briefly.
  7. Pour and spread over cream cheese layer.
  8. Refrigerate until set.
