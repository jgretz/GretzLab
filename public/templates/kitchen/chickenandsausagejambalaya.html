Chicken and Sausage Jambalaya

Yields 10-12 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Chicken Breast
  • 1 lb Andouille Sausage
  • 1 Green Bell Pepper
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3 Ribs Celery
  • 3 Cloves Garlic
  • 32 oz Diced Tomatoes
  • 16 oz Chicken Stock
  • 1.25 cup Long Grain Rice
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • 1 tsp Basil
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • 1 tsp Sage
  • 1 tsp Paprika
  • 1 tsp Creole Seasoning
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • .5 tsp Pepper

Instructions

  1. Brown sausage
  2. Add veggies, reduce until softened
  3. Add chicken and Worcestershire sauce, cook until outside of chicken is white
  4. Add spices and broth
  5. Bring to boil
  6. Add Rice, bring back up to boil
  7. Reduce heat to low, cover, let sit for 30 minutes - 1 hour, until rice is wet but not soupy
