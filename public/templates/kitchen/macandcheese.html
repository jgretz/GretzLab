Mac & Cheese

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 5.5 cup milk
  • .5 cup flour
  • 2 tsp kosher salt
  • .25 tsp nutmeg
  • .25 tsp black pepper
  • .25 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 4.5 cup cheddar
  • 1 cup Gruyere
  • .5 cup Pecorino Ramano
  • 1 lb shells

Instructions

  1. Melt butter, add flour, stir until you have a roux
  2. At the same time warm milk, add to roux
  3. Stir in spices
  4. Slowly add the cheese (continue stirring to avoid clumping, leave a little cheddar to the side)
  5. Boil water, and prep shells
  6. Combine cheese mixture and shells
  7. Sprinkle remaining cheddar on top
  8. Bake @ 375 until top is brown (30 minutes)
