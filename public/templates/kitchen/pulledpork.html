Smoked Pulled Pork

Yields 6-8 Servings

Equipment

  • Smoker

Ingredients

  • 5 lbs Pork Butt
  • .33 cup Rub
  • 1 cup Yellow Mustard

Rub

Yields 3 cups of rub
  • .75 cup brown sugar
  • .75 cup white sugar
  • .5 cup paprika
  • .25 cup kosher salt
  • .25 cup garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp ginger powder
  • 2 tbsp onion powder
  • 2 tsp rosemary powder
  • 2 tsp celery seed powder

Instructions

  1. Rub down pork butt with mustard until all is covered lightly
  2. Add rub until covered
  3. Wrap in foil, refrigerate overnight
  4. Smoke butt in smoker at 225 for 8-10 hours until pork is 195
  5. Remove pork, wrap in foil, let rest for 20 - 30 minutes
  6. Pull pork apart with bare hands
