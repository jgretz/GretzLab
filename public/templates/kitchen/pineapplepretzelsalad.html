Pineapple Pretzel Salad

Yields 6-8 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cup crushed pretzels
  • 1 cup butter
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 8 oz whipped topping
  • 20 oz pineapple
  • 3.4 oz vanilla pudding mix

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Mix melted butter and pretzels.
  3. Spray pan with spray, flatten in pretzels
  4. Bake for 10 minutes
  5. Cream together cream cheese and sugar. Fold in whipped topping. Spread over cooled crust.
  6. Combine pineapple and pudding mix. Spread over whipped topping layer.
